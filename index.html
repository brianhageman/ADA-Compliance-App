Accessibility Workflow for Teachers

Upload a class document, review the fixes, and download the improved copy.

Designed for simple teacher workflows: upload notes, slides, handouts, and worksheets, let the app fix the safest accessibility issues automatically, then review anything that still needs human judgment before downloading the updated file.

How it works

  1. Upload a Word or PowerPoint file.
  2. Review the audit summary and teacher review queue.
  3. Download the remediated copy and accessibility report.

Current best support: `.docx` and `.pptx`.
